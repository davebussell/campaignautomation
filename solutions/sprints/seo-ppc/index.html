SEO/PPC Opportunity Sprint

2–3 Week Sprint · $5,000–$10,000

Stop paying for clicks you already earn organically.

We analyse your paid and organic data together to find where you're cannibalizing your own rankings and where content gaps are costing you CPCs you shouldn't be paying.

Book the SEO/PPC Sprint →

What you upload

  • Google Ads search terms export
  • GA4 landing page performance
  • Search Console query report (last 3 months)
  • Competitor domain list (optional)

What you get back

Keyword Overlap Analysis

Which queries you rank and pay for simultaneously — and what that overlap is costing you.

Cannibalisation Report

Pages where your own ads are suppressing organic CTR and eroding long-term search equity.

Content Gap Map

High-intent queries with no organic coverage — where you're paying for clicks you could earn for free.

Landing Page Brief Pack

SEO-optimised briefs for the top 5 gap opportunities, ready for your writers or AI tools.

Negative Keyword List

Paid terms to suppress where organic already wins — stops you bidding against yourself.

Channel Attribution Model

A clear framework for which conversions belong to SEO vs. PPC — so each channel gets credit.

How it works

01
Scope

Upload your search terms export, GA4 data, and Search Console report via a secure link.

02
Analyse

We run keyword overlap analysis and identify where paid and organic intersect — and conflict.

03
Map

We build the cannibalisation report, content gap map, and channel opportunity matrix.

04
Hand off

You receive the brief pack, negative keyword list, and attribution model with a live walkthrough.
