2–3 Week Sprint · $5,000–$10,000

Turn your ad account export into a prioritized action plan.

Upload your Google Ads CSV export. Get a waste analysis, a negative keyword build, a budget reallocation plan, and ongoing guardrail automation rules — all handed over with AGENTS.md and prompt packs ready to run.

Book the PPC Intelligence Sprint →

What you upload

  • Google Ads campaign export (CSV)
  • Search terms report
  • GA4 landing page performance data
  • Search Console data (optional but recommended)

What you get back

Waste Analysis Report

Line-by-line breakdown of where spend is leaking — by campaign, ad group, and search term.

Negative Keyword Build

A curated negative list across match types, ready to upload directly into your Google Ads account.

Budget Reallocation Plan

A prioritized shift of budget from underperforming to high-intent campaigns with projected impact.

Guardrail Automation Rules

Google Ads automated rules and scripts configured to flag anomalies and enforce spend caps going forward.

PPC AGENTS.md

Your account's persistent agent instruction file — roles, tools, guardrails, and output formats defined.

Prompt Pack for Ongoing Use

Reusable Claude prompts for monthly audits, search term reviews, and bid strategy evaluations.

How it works

01
Upload your files
