Home/Solutions/Automation Sprints/Lead Reactivation Sprint
3–4 Week Sprint · $8,000–$15,000

Turn your dormant CRM into a reactivation pipeline.

Most companies have 12–18 months of leads they've never properly followed up on. This sprint segments them, builds a personalization engine, creates reactivation sequences, and wires up the routing logic — so cold leads become active conversations again.

Book the Lead Reactivation Sprint →

What you upload

  • CRM lead export (CSV) — all dormant contacts, 90+ days no activity
  • Lead source and campaign attribution data
  • Previous email performance data (open rates, clicks, bounces)
  • ICP definition — firmographic and behavioural signals

What you get back

Segmentation Model

Leads scored and clustered by recency, source, ICP fit, and inferred intent — documented and repeatable.

Personalization Rules Engine

Logic that maps segment attributes to message variables — industry, role, pain signal, and offer angle.

3 Reactivation Sequences

High-ICP, mid-fit, and long-dormant tracks — each with 4–6 touchpoints, subject lines, and body copy.

Routing Logic + AGENTS.md

Rules that route engaged leads to sales and unresponsive leads to a nurture or suppression list automatically.

Trigger / Action / Impact Log Setup

A structured logging framework so every sequence action is auditable — open, click, reply, routing outcome.

How it works

01
CRM upload + segmentation — Week 1

We ingest your export, score leads against your ICP, and cluster into reactivation segments with documented logic.

02
Personalization engine — Week 2

Rules mapping segment attributes to message variables are built and tested against a sample set.

03
Sequence build — Week 3

All three reactivation sequences are written, reviewed, and formatted for import into your CRM or email platform.

04
Routing + handover — Week 4

Routing logic is wired up, impact logging is configured, and AGENTS.md is packaged for ongoing use by your team.
