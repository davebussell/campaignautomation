2 Week Sprint · $5,000–$10,000

From campaign idea to full launch kit — in two weeks with AI.

Stop spending 3 weeks manually building campaigns. This sprint produces a complete, ready-to-launch campaign kit — ad groups, copy variants, landing page outlines, UTM plan, and QA checklist — built and documented so your team can launch and iterate on their own.

Book the Campaign Launch Sprint →

What's in the kit

Campaign Brief

AI-generated strategic brief covering offer, audience, intent signals, competitive angle, and success metrics.

Ad Group Structure

Fully built ad group architecture organized by theme, intent tier, and match type — ready to upload.

Copy Variants

3 copy variants per ad group — headlines, descriptions, and responsive ad assets — reviewed and formatted.

Landing Page Outline

Section-by-section LP outline mapped to each ad group's intent, with headline hooks and CTA direction.

UTM Taxonomy Plan

A consistent, documented UTM convention for every campaign element — clean data from day one.

Launch QA Checklist

Pre-launch review checklist covering tracking, copy, targeting, budget, and landing page parity.

AGENTS.md for Ongoing Use

Your campaign's persistent agent file — so Claude knows your offer, audience, and guardrails on every future run.

How it works

01
Brief & brand upload

Share your offer details, ICP definition, and any existing brand or tone-of-voice documentation.

02
AI brief generation — Days 1–3

We run the brief generation workflow and align on campaign intent clusters before building begins.

03
Copy + structure build — Week 1

Ad group structure and all copy variants are built, reviewed, and formatted for upload.

04
LP outlines + UTM + QA — Week 2

Landing page outlines, UTM taxonomy, and QA checklist are finalized. AGENTS.md is packaged for handover.
