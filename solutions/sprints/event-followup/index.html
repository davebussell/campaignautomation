Event Follow-Up Sprint

2–3 Week Sprint · $5,000–$10,000

Turn event leads into pipeline within 48 hours — automatically.

Most event leads go cold in the 3–5 days it takes to manually process them. We build an automated capture-to-pipeline workflow that fires within hours of a badge scan or form fill.

Book the Event Follow-Up Sprint →

What you upload

  • Badge scan export or event registration CSV
  • CRM field mapping doc
  • Existing email sequences (if any)
  • ICP definition

What you get back

Lead Capture Automation

Badge or form data routed into your CRM in real-time — no manual imports, no spreadsheet handoffs.

Enrichment Workflow

Company, title, and LinkedIn data appended automatically the moment a lead enters the system.

Personalisation Engine

Leads segmented by role, company size, and event session attended — before the first email sends.

Sequence Templates

3-touch follow-up sequence per segment, with Claude-generated copy tailored to each ICP slice.

Pipeline Routing Rules

Qualified leads to sales, nurture to marketing, unqualified to list — automatically, with audit trail.

How it works

01
Capture setup

We configure your badge scan or form trigger to push leads into CRM within minutes of collection.

02
Enrichment config

Enrichment tools are connected and field mapping validated against your CRM schema.

03
Sequence build

Personalised follow-up sequences are drafted, reviewed, and loaded into your email platform per segment.

04
Routing logic

Scoring thresholds and routing rules are set. Sales gets the hot leads. Marketing gets the rest. No manual triage.
