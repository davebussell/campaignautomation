What the Audit sprint delivers

Six concrete outputs you own, with no fluff and no vague recommendations. Everything here scopes the sprints that follow.

Current-state workflow review

A documented map of how your campaigns are planned, launched, and optimised today, and where the friction lives.

PPC waste analysis

Search term audit, match-type efficiency review, and estimated monthly waste with a prioritised fix list.

SEO/PPC opportunity map

Keyword gaps, cannibalization flags, and quick-win opportunities across paid and organic together.

AI automation roadmap

A ranked list of automation opportunities specific to your stack, team size, and budget. Not a generic checklist.

Claude/Cursor setup recommendations

Practical configuration advice so your team can start using AI tools on campaigns from day one.

30-day action plan

Week-by-week priorities your team can execute immediately, and yours to keep whether or not we work together.

Our performance commitment

The Audit does not end with a vague list of ideas. We read your accounts, find where time and money are leaking, and recommend a short list of sprints. Every sprint we recommend comes with a measurable target tied to your own numbers, written down before we build anything.

If we cannot define a target we believe in, we will not recommend the sprint. That keeps us honest, and it keeps you from paying for work that cannot show its return.

Hours back

Time saved, counted

We baseline the hours your team spends on a workflow today, then set a target reduction once it runs as a system.

ROAS

Return on ad spend

For paid media we set a target lift against your current ROAS, driven by negative-keyword, budget, and regional automation.

Quality Score

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a Readiness Score first?

No, but it helps. The free Readiness Score takes about four minutes and gives us your tier and binding constraint up front, so the Audit sprint goes deeper, faster. The Audit itself is the first sprint we run together once you decide to engage.

Do I need to give you account access?

Yes, read-only. We need view-only access to your key accounts (Google Ads, GA4, Search Console) to pull the data and ground the plan in your real numbers. Read-only means we can see but never change anything, we never take admin or edit rights, and you can revoke access the moment the audit is done. Prefer to share exports for some accounts? That works too.

How long does it take?

We confirm your request within 2 business days and set up read-only access (or send an export checklist if you prefer). Once we have the data, allow about 5 business days for the full review and your findings call.

What happens after the Audit sprint?

You get a live call to walk through the findings and a prioritised 30-day plan. From there we build the sprints the audit identifies, the highest-ROI workflows in order, with your team. There's no pressure to continue, but most teams do.
