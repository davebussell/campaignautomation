Frequently asked questions

Is it really free? What's the catch?

It's genuinely free, and there's no catch. The audit is how we introduce ourselves and prove we can help before you spend anything. If you take the 30-day plan and run it yourself, that's a great outcome. If you'd rather we build it with you, that's what Automation Sprints are for — but there's no obligation.

Do I need to give you account access?

No. The audit is file-based. You export data from Google Ads, GA4, and Search Console yourself and share it after you request the audit. We never request login credentials, OAuth access, or admin permissions to your accounts.

How long does it take?

We confirm your request within 2 business days and send your export checklist. Once we have your files, allow about 5 business days for the full review and your findings call.

What happens after the audit?

You get a live call to walk through the findings. From there, the 30-day plan is yours to execute independently, or you can move into an Automation Sprint where we build the priority workflow with your team. There's no pressure either way.
