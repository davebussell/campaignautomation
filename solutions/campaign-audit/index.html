Frequently asked questions

Do I need to give you account access?

No. The audit is file-based. You export data from Google Ads, GA4, and Search Console yourself and upload it via our intake form. We never request login credentials, OAuth access, or admin permissions to your accounts.

How long does it take?

From the moment you submit your files, allow 5 business days for the full report. We'll send a confirmation within 24 hours of your upload and a heads-up the day before delivery.

What happens after the audit?

You get a 60-minute review call to walk through findings. From there, the 30-day plan is yours to execute independently, or you can move into an Automation Sprint where we build the priority workflow with your team.
