How a sprint works

01
Audit recommendation

Every sprint is scoped from your Campaign Automation Audit findings — we build what the data says will have the highest impact first.

02
Scope & kickoff

We agree on inputs, outputs, guardrails, and success criteria before a single line of prompt is written. No scope creep.

03
Build sprint (2–4 weeks)

Weekly check-ins, shared working files, and staged delivery — you see progress throughout, not just at the end.

04
Handover + training

Half-day training session with your team, full documentation, and a 30-day check-in call included.
