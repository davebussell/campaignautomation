How a sprint works

01
Score + prep

Your Readiness Score determines which sprints you're eligible for. Leapfrog sprints start at any score. Integration sprints are scoped after the Audit.

02
Scope & kickoff

We agree on inputs, outputs, guardrails, and success criteria before a single line of prompt is written. No undefined scope, no scope creep.

03
Build sprint (2–4 weeks)

Weekly check-ins, shared working files, and staged delivery, so you see progress throughout, not just at the end.

04
Handover + training

Half-day training session with your team, full documentation, and a 30-day check-in call included in every sprint.
