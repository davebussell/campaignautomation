What we look for

  • 5+ years deep in one area — paid, SEO, RevOps, lifecycle, data, agents, or governance
  • Hands-on with a major platform: Google, Meta, Microsoft, Salesforce, HubSpot, or Marketo
  • You build with guardrails — approvals, logging, reversibility — not black boxes
  • Comfortable scoping and shipping inside a 2–4 week sprint

What you get

  • Sprint-based work matched to your skills, with clear scopes and transparent rates
  • Clients who already understand governed automation — less educating, more building
  • A bench of specialists to collaborate with across disciplines
  • We handle sales and account management; you focus on the build
