Tomás Rivera

Paid Search Scientist
Newark, NJ · he/him · 11 yrs · Paid media

Bid scripts that decide like a strategist, log like an auditor

This is an illustrative profile of a role we’re actively filling — a representative archetype, not a current team member. If it describes you, apply for this role.

The profile

Tomás Rivera writes the bid and budget scripts that take guesswork out of paid search. After eleven years in the channel, he stopped trusting rule-of-thumb account management and started encoding the rules instead, in Google Ads Scripts that read from GA4, surface decisions, and leave a paper trail behind every change.

He builds for advertisers who want the lift of automated bidding without handing the account to a black box. His scripts work as Smart Bidding alternatives: explicit logic, thresholds you set, and a record of what moved and why. Big shifts pause for approval; small ones proceed and get logged.

The guardrails are not optional, because an unwatched script can drain a daily budget before lunch. Tomás caps how far automation can swing in a single run, writes every adjustment to a Looker dashboard, and keeps each action reversible, so the account stays accountable to a human, not just efficient.

What this builder owns

Threshold-gated bid adjustment script

A Google Ads Script that shifts bids within set bands, holds any change above your limit for approval, and writes every move to a log.

GA4-driven budget pacing monitor

Pulls conversion and spend signals from GA4 to flag overpacing campaigns and throttle budgets before a daily cap is blown.

Smart Bidding alternative with audit trail

Rules-based bidding logic that replaces opaque automated strategies with explicit conditions and a full record of each adjustment.

Looker change-and-decision dashboard

A Looker view that surfaces every automated bid and budget action, what triggered it, and a one-click reference to reverse it.
