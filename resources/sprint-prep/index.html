Before you sprint

Build your sprint foundation.

The difference between a sprint that delivers and one that stalls is almost always preparation. This page tells you exactly what to have in place — organized by your Readiness Score tier and company stage.

Prep track by tier

What you need at each stage

Your active track is highlighted when you've completed the Readiness Score. Each track builds on the one below it — you don't need to complete a lower track before starting a Leapfrog Sprint.

Siloed · Score 0–49

Foundation work first
  • Complete the free Readiness Score
  • Document your current campaign workflow
  • Export your last 90 days of campaign data
  • Identify who owns campaign decisions
  • Map which tools each team member uses daily
  • Book a Campaign Automation Audit
Take the Readiness Score →

Emerging · Score 50–69

Foundation sprints available
  • Set up Claude (claude.ai or API access)
  • Download the Campaign Brief prompt pack
  • Document your campaign naming conventions
  • Identify your one highest-friction workflow
  • Review the AI Brief Machine sprint
  • Complete the Audit to unlock integration sprints
See Leapfrog Sprints →

Operational · Score 70–84

Integration sprints available
  • Map your full stack (ad platforms, CRM, analytics)
  • Verify conversion tracking across all channels
  • Download the stack audit template
  • Document your data ownership + access rights
  • Set up a shared prompt library for your team
  • Request a sprint scoping call
Request a sprint →

Agentic · Score 85–100

Intelligence layer ready
  • Download the AGENTS.md + CLAUDE.md templates
  • Map your orchestration architecture
  • Define your agent trigger + reporting cadence
  • Identify measurement baseline for agentic output
  • Explore the Campaign Strategy Portal
  • Request a Portal or advanced sprint scoping call
Explore the Strategy Portal →

Where to go from here

Pre / Seed
Start with a Leapfrog Sprint — no prerequisites needed

At pre-revenue or seed stage, data infrastructure takes a back seat. Leapfrog Sprints let you build real AI capability — briefs, intelligence pipelines, content systems — without waiting for data maturity. Pick one. Build it. Own it.

See Leapfrog Sprints →
Early Stage
Leapfrog now, Audit when you're ready to integrate

At $0–$1M ARR you're building process while running campaigns. Leapfrog Sprints systematize your highest-friction workflows immediately. When you're ready to connect your stack, the Audit scopes your first integration sprint.

View all sprints →
Growth
Audit first, then pick the integration sprint with highest ROI

At $1M–$10M ARR you have enough campaign volume and stack depth for integration sprints to compound. The Audit identifies which workflow — PPC Intelligence, Launch Kit, Content Brief — delivers the fastest measurable return for your current situation.

Learn about the Audit →
Scale
Lead Reactivation, Event Follow-Up, and the Strategy Portal

At $10M+ you have the data depth and org structure for high-complexity integration sprints — and the pipeline volume to make Lead Reactivation and Event Follow-Up measurably valuable. The Campaign Strategy Portal gives you an ongoing AI intelligence layer on top.
