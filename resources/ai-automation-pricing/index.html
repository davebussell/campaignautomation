Common questions

FAQ.

Do you offer retainers?

No, the portal is our recurring offer. Sprints are project-based with a fixed scope and timeline — not ongoing retainers. The portal gives you month-to-month flexibility without a long-term commitment.

What if I need multiple sprints?

You can run sprints sequentially. Most clients start with an Audit, then run 2–3 sprints in the first year. Each sprint targets a distinct workflow, so they stack without overlap.

Is there a setup fee?

No. The sprint price includes everything — scoping, build, guardrail configuration, prompt packs, AGENTS.md, and the training session. What you see is what you pay.

What's your refund policy?

Audits are non-refundable once delivered. Sprint payments are milestone-based — you pay in stages tied to deliverables, not upfront in full. The portal can be cancelled at any time before the next billing cycle.
