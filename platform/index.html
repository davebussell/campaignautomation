Expectations

What the portal does not do

Knowing what a tool won't do is as important as knowing what it will. The portal is a strategy and intelligence layer — not an execution engine.

Push changes to ad accounts
The portal generates recommendations and assets. Your team reviews and applies them. No autonomous ad changes, ever.
Replace Semrush, Ahrefs, HubSpot, or GA4
It reads your exports from those tools. It does not replicate their functionality or replace your existing stack.
Build a data warehouse
Data is uploaded monthly as flat files. There is no live data pipeline, API sync, or long-term data storage.
Complex bid automation
Bid strategy recommendations are part of the audit and sprint layer — not automated through the portal.

Build roadmap

The portal is being built incrementally — each phase adds capability while keeping the human-in-the-loop model intact.

01
M1–3

Manual review
