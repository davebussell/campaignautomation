How ready is your team to automate campaign execution?

The Campaign Automation Readiness Score assesses 12 areas of your campaign operation and returns a 0–100 score, your top three bottlenecks, and a recommended sprint — so you know exactly where to start.

What you'll learn

Your score (0–100)

A single number that reflects how mature your campaign automation capability is across planning, execution, reporting, and tooling.

Top 3 bottlenecks

The specific areas where your team is losing the most time or leaving the most automation opportunity on the table.

Recommended sprint

Based on your score and bottleneck profile, we'll recommend the Automation Sprint most likely to generate the fastest return.

Claude/Cursor setup guide

A personalised quickstart guide for getting your team using AI tools on campaigns — matched to your current toolstack and skill level.
